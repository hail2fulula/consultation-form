How It Works

Our structured, documentation-led process is designed to give your case the strongest possible foundation. Here's what to expect from start to finish.

1

Free Case Review

Submit your details through our consultation form or call us directly. A case specialist will review your situation within 24 hours, assess the nature of the fraud, and determine whether your case qualifies for our services. There is no cost or obligation at this stage.

2

Evidence Collection

Once your case is accepted, we guide you through the documentation process. This includes gathering transaction records, communication logs, platform screenshots, and any other relevant materials. Our team helps organize everything into a structured case file.

3

Investigation & Strategy

Our analysts and legal partners examine the fraud entity, trace fund movements where possible, and develop a tailored recovery strategy. For cryptocurrency cases, we collaborate with blockchain forensic specialists to follow the digital trail.

4

Engagement & Pursuit

We initiate contact with the relevant financial institutions, payment processors, and regulatory bodies. Our legal network prepares formal demands and, where applicable, coordinates with law enforcement agencies to strengthen the pressure on fraudulent entities.

5

Resolution & Recovery

When funds are successfully traced and recovered, they are returned to you through verified channels. We keep you informed at every stage of the process and provide full transparency on the progress of your case. Timelines vary depending on case complexity.
