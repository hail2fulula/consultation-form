Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you have questions before getting started. Here are answers to the most common ones we receive.

What does the recovery process involve? ▼

Our process begins with a free case review where we assess your situation. If we determine your case is viable, we move into evidence gathering, investigation, and active pursuit of your funds through financial institutions, payment processors, and regulatory channels. Each case is different, and we tailor our approach accordingly.

How long does the process typically take? ▼

Timelines vary significantly based on the complexity of the case, the type of fraud involved, the jurisdictions in play, and the responsiveness of financial institutions. Some cases resolve within weeks, while more complex international matters may take several months. We provide realistic timeframe estimates during your initial consultation.

What types of fraud do you handle? ▼

We work with clients affected by forex trading scams, cryptocurrency fraud, romance scams, binary options schemes, phishing attacks, employment fraud, and other forms of online financial deception. If you're unsure whether your situation qualifies, we encourage you to reach out — the initial review is free.

What are your fees? ▼

The initial case review is always free and comes with no obligations. If we take on your case, our fee structure is explained clearly before you commit to anything. We believe in full transparency — there are no hidden costs or surprise charges at any point in the process.

What documents do I need to provide? ▼

Helpful documentation includes transaction records (bank statements, transfer confirmations), communication logs with the fraudulent entity (emails, chat messages), screenshots of the platform or website, and any account details or reference numbers. Don't worry if you don't have everything — we'll guide you through what's needed.

Is my personal information protected? ▼

Absolutely. We take data security seriously. All client information is handled in accordance with Canadian privacy legislation. Your personal details and case materials are stored securely and are only shared with relevant parties as required for the recovery process, with your consent.

Can you guarantee that my funds will be recovered? ▼

No legitimate recovery service can guarantee a specific outcome. What we can promise is a thorough, professional effort backed by experienced investigators and legal professionals. We provide honest assessments of each case's viability and keep you informed throughout the entire process.

Can you help if the scam originated outside of Canada? ▼

Yes. Online fraud frequently involves international entities. We have a network of partners across multiple jurisdictions that enables us to pursue cross-border cases. During your consultation, we'll assess the international aspects of your situation and explain how they affect the recovery approach.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is happy to discuss your specific situation. Reach out for a free, confidential consultation.
